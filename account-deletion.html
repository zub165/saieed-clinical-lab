Account & Data Deletion

Last updated: June 26, 2026

App name: Lab Management (Saeed Lab)

Android package: com.saiedlab.mobile

Developer contact: zub165@yahoo.com

Privacy policy: privacy.html

If you created a staff account in the Lab Management app, you can request account deletion and deletion of data associated with that account using the options below. You do not need to create a new account to submit a deletion request.

1. Account deletion

Permanently delete your staff login and the app account tied to your username/email.

Option A — In the app (recommended)

  1. Open Lab Management (Saeed Lab) on your device.
  2. Sign in with your staff account.
  3. Go to Settings → Delete account (or Request account deletion).
  4. Follow the on-screen steps to confirm your request.

Option B — By email (without using the app)

  1. Email zub165@yahoo.com
  2. Subject: Account Deletion Request - SAEED Lab App
  3. Include: registered email, username, and lab/branch name (if known)

Processing time: Verified account deletion requests are completed within 30 days.

2. Data deletion

When your account is deleted, we delete or de-identify personal data linked to that account, including staff profile data on our servers and cached session data on your device.

Data deleted with account deletion:

  • Staff username, email, role, and login credentials
  • App preferences and local cached staff session data on your device
  • Server-side staff profile records associated with your account

Request deletion of specific data only

Email zub165@yahoo.com with subject Data Deletion Request - SAEED Lab App and describe what to delete. We verify your identity before processing.

Data we may retain

  • Patient/lab records may remain under your laboratory’s legal record-keeping obligations.
  • Payment records may be retained as required by Stripe, Google Play, and applicable law.
  • Backups: Deleted data may persist in encrypted backups briefly, then is purged on rotation.

3. Questions

Email zub165@yahoo.com. We aim to reply within 48 hours.
