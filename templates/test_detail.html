About this Test

The Comprehensive Health Profile is our most popular panel, designed to give you a complete snapshot of your overall health. This panel includes tests for cholesterol, blood sugar, kidney and liver function, complete blood count, and other key health indicators.

This test is ideal for those who want a comprehensive assessment of their current health status, or for those who are monitoring existing health conditions. With one simple blood draw, you'll receive detailed information about multiple aspects of your health.

Tests Included

  • Complete Blood Count (CBC)
    Includes WBC, RBC, Hemoglobin, etc.
  • Comprehensive Metabolic Panel (CMP)
    Includes Glucose, Kidney, Liver tests
  • Lipid Panel
    Total Cholesterol, HDL, LDL, Triglycerides
  • Hemoglobin A1c
    3-month average blood sugar
  • Thyroid Stimulating Hormone (TSH)
    Thyroid function screening
  • Total Vitamin D
    25-Hydroxy Vitamin D levels
  • Iron and Total Iron Binding Capacity
    Iron status assessment

Who should get this test?

This comprehensive panel is ideal for anyone who wants a thorough assessment of their overall health status. It's particularly recommended for:

  • Adults who have not had lab work done in over a year
  • Those with family history of heart disease, diabetes, or other chronic conditions
  • People looking to establish baseline health measurements
  • Individuals monitoring existing health conditions
  • Those taking medications that require regular monitoring

Preparation

For most accurate results, please follow these guidelines before your test:

  • Fast for 12 hours before your test (water is permitted)
  • Take medications as prescribed unless instructed otherwise by your healthcare provider
  • Avoid alcohol consumption for 24 hours before testing
  • Avoid strenuous exercise for 24 hours before testing
